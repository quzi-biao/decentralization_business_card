Data Storage & Security

1. Storage Method

Decentralized Storage:

  • Local Storage: Your core data is encrypted and stored on your device
  • Distributed Storage: Large files like images and videos are stored on decentralized networks (e.g., MinIO)
  • No Centralized Servers: We don't store your personal information on centralized servers

2. Security Measures

  • End-to-End Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data transmission
  • Key Management: Encryption keys are managed by you (mnemonic phrase)
  • Access Control: Customize privacy settings for each field
  • Secure Transmission: All network transfers use HTTPS protocol

3. Data Retention

  • Data is permanently stored on your device until you delete it
  • You can export or clear all data at any time
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered (unless you have a backup)
