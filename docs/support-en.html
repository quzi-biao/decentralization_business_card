üöÄ Quick Start

1. Create Your Digital Identity

  1. Open the app for the first time and tap "Create My Identity"
  2. The system will automatically generate encryption key pairs and mnemonic phrase
  3. Important: Please save your mnemonic phrase securely - it's the only way to recover your identity
  4. After backup, tap "I've Backed Up, Start Using"

2. Edit Your Business Card

  1. Go to "My Card" page
  2. Fill in basic information: name, position, company, etc.
  3. Upload avatar and company images
  4. Add contact information and social media accounts
  5. Set privacy permissions to control information visibility

3. Exchange Business Cards

  1. Tap "Exchange Cards" to enter the scanning page
  2. Generate your QR code for others to scan
  3. Or scan someone else's QR code to get their card
  4. After successful exchange, cards are automatically saved to contacts

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I forget my mnemonic phrase?

A: The mnemonic phrase is the only way to recover your identity. If lost, it cannot be recovered. We recommend:

  • Write down the mnemonic phrase on paper and store it in a safe place
  • Do not take screenshots or save it electronically
  • Regularly check that your backup is intact

Q: How do I switch devices?

A: On your new device:

  1. Install the AI Business Card app
  2. Select "Restore Identity"
  3. Enter your mnemonic phrase
  4. The system will automatically restore your identity and data

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: Your data uses decentralized storage:

  • Core data is encrypted and stored on local devices
  • Large files like images are stored on decentralized storage networks
  • You can export or delete all data at any time

Q: How do I set privacy permissions?

A: On the "Access Control" page:

  • Select the field you want to set (e.g., phone, email)
  • Toggle the "Privacy" switch
  • Fields set as private will not be shared when exchanging cards

Q: How do I use the AI assistant?

A: Go to the "AI Assistant" page:

  • Type or voice ask questions
  • AI will answer based on your business card data
  • Supports querying contacts, analyzing networks, and more

Q: Is the app free?

A: Core features are completely free, including:

  • Unlimited card creation and exchange
  • Basic AI assistant features
  • Data storage and backup
